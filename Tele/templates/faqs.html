Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about using our platform.

Afiabora is a health platform that allows you to track symptoms, book appointments, and view your health history. It provides tools to manage your health and improve your well-being.

You can book an appointment by going to the Appointment section, selecting your preferred doctor, and choosing a suitable time slot. You will receive a confirmation once your appointment is booked.

Your health reports can be accessed from the Reports section. Here, you can view and download your medical reports, such as test results and doctor notes.

Yes, you can update your health information from the Dashboard. You can enter new symptoms, record changes in weight, and other vital health data.

The symptom checker can be found in the Dashboard. By answering a series of questions, you can get an idea of what health issues you may be facing.
